About Us


Welcome to The stake House, where we serve the finest steaks in town. We're passionate about meat, and we're committed to providing our customers with an unforgettable dining experience. We start with only the highest quality beef, sourced from local farms. Our steaks are dry-aged for at least 28 days, which tenderizes the meat and enhances its flavor. We then cook our steaks to perfection over an open flame, using a combination of hardwood charcoal and hickory wood. In addition to our steaks, we also offer a variety of other delicious menu items, including fresh seafood, appetizers, and desserts. We have a full bar, where you can enjoy a classic cocktail or a glass of wine from our extensive wine list. Our atmosphere is warm and inviting, and our staff is friendly and attentive. We're the perfect place to enjoy a special occasion dinner or a casual meal with friends and family.
